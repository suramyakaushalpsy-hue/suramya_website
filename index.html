I believe that meaningful psychological care lies at the intersection of compassion, clinical expertise, and ethical responsibility. Therapy is not about “fixing” individuals, but about supporting insight, self-awareness, and sustainable change.

Guided by the principle “सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः” — May all beings be happy — I aim to support individuals in their journey toward emotional well-being, self-understanding, and personal growth, recognizing that the good life is a process, not a state of being.

Professional Background

I am a Clinical Psychologist registered with Rehabilitation Council of India(RCI), with experience across pediatric and adult mental health, working in hospital and clinical settings. My work involves comprehensive psychological assessment, psychotherapy, and evidence-based interventions, with a strong focus on understanding individuals beyond diagnostic labels and presenting concerns.

I strive to create a therapeutic space that is safe, collaborative, and respectful, allowing clients to explore their experiences with honesty and self-compassion.

When to Reach out to a Therapist

Signs that it's time for professional help

  • Feeling overwhelmed by stress or anxiety
  • Struggling with persistent sadness or mood swings
  • Difficulty managing relationships or work-life balance
  • Experiencing trauma or major life changes
  • Changes in sleep, appetite, or motivation
  • Child or teen struggling with behavior or emotions
  • Seeking personal growth and self-awareness
